Hotel Reservation

We provide two options, Wenjin Hotel or Holiday Inn Beijing Haidian, for accomodation. While Wenjin Hotel (about 12 minutes on foot from the hotel to the conference venue) is located closer to Tsinghua University than Holiday Inn Beijing Haidian (about 20 minutes on foot from the hotel to the conference venue), the price of a single room in Wenjin Hotel may be a little bit higher than that in Holiday Inn Beijing Haidian. High speed Internet access and the continental breakfast are included. For individual reservations, please contact with Ms. WANG Yu (email:wangy36@sem.tsinghua.edu.cn) asap. In order to qualify for the group rate and to ensure availability, please make your hotel reservation prior to May 1, 2019.

About Wenjin Hotel
(http://wenjin.com.cn/)
Wenjin Hotel is located in TusPark which is known as “China’s Silicon Valley”, in the heart of Zhongguancun District, Beijing. The hotel borders Tsinghua University in the north, Peking University in the west, and very close to the Headquarters of Microsoft, Google and Sohu. It is close to the Summer Palace, also the Garden of Perfection and Brightness.

The hotel offers 399 capacious and comfortable guest rooms which make you feel at home. The food and beverage facilities including Chinese restaurant, Cafe, traditional Korean restaurant and characteristic Bars will offer you all kinds of dainty and surely bring you unforgettable memories of pleasure of dining. Indoor swimming pool, Gym, Tianchang Golf Club and the professional Spa from Singapore provide you with ideal choices of refreshment.

The hotel has an ideal location with 5 minutes’ walk to the nearest train station and 30 minutes’ drive to the airport. Neighboring the Olympic Village, it only takes 10 minutes’ driving to National Stadium. The hotel’s geographic location is North Latitude 39.59 and East Longitude 116.19.

About Holiday Inn Beijing Haidian
(https://www.ihg.com/holidayinn/hotels/gb/en/beijing/pegsh/hoteldetail)
Holiday Inn Beijing Haidian is a grand 4-star property featuring contemporary rooms in a business area of Beijing. Holiday Inn Beijing Haidian operates since 2011.

This hotel is about 13 km away from Si De Park. Such tourist places as a temple, a palace and a sanctuary are fairly close by. Set next to bars and a stadium, this hotel also offers a heated indoor pool, a ballroom and a golf course on site. Within a 10-minute walk guests will find Wudaokou subway station. The hotel is located within 25 km distance of Beijing Capital Int. Airport.

Holiday Inn Beijing Haidian offers accommodation in 291 guestrooms complete with an in-room safe, climate control, flat-screen television, a trouser press and a work desk. Guests can make use of a hairdryer, dressing gowns and toiletries. There is a restaurant for guests to enjoy their meal. Asian dishes are served in the specialty restaurant. The trendy authentic bar offers billiards and a terrace. Guests can eat at Double Forest Restaurant and Fire Fire, situated within a 10-minute walk of the venue. The hotel pampers guests with a health club, a Jacuzzi and a sauna as well as steam baths and massage. On-site fitness facilities include a gym and a fitness center. There are currency exchange, a baggage storage and a lift available on the premises.
