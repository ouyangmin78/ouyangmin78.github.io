Tsinghua Workshop in Macroeconomics 2020

Keynote Speakers:
Dirk Krueger and Atif Mian

The 12th annual Tsinghua Workshop in Macroeconomics, sponsored by the National Institute for Fiscal Studies of the Tsinghua University and the Cheung Kong Graduate School of Business, will be held on June 15-17, 2020 in Beijing, China. We welcome papers in all areas related to macroeconomics. Each paper will be given about an hour for presentation and will be assigned a discussant. If you are interested in presenting at the conference, please submit your paper at:
http://editorialexpress.com/conference/twm2020/
The deadline for submission is Sunday, March 1, 2020. You can check detailed information on the conference including past programs at:
http://www.twmacro.com

If you have any question, please contact the organizers by sending an email to minouyang2018@gmail.com.
